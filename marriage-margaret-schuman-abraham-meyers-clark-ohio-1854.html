Marriage record of Margaret Schuman and Abraham Myers of Clark, Ohio in 1854

Transcript (and link to online image of original document) for the marriage record of Margaret Schuman and Abraham Myers in Clark, Ohio, United States from 1854.

Citation and File

Marriage of Margaret Schuman and Abraham Myers, 26 November 1854, Clark County Probate Court; Marriage Record Book 4, page 102; Ohio, United States (https://familysearch.org/pal:/MM9.3.1/TH-1-17958-646-41).

Transcript

Abraham Myers
&
Margaret Schuman
License issued Nov 25/54

The State of Ohio, Clark county, Probate Court

Personally appeared before the Judge of said Court,
Abraham Myers, who being duly sworn, deposeth and saith,
that he is more than twenty-one years of age; that Margaret Schuman
is more than eighteen years of age; that they are both unmarried; not nearer
of kin than first cousins; and that she is a resident of said county.

Subscribed and affirmed [sworn is crossed out] to, before me,
this 26th day of November, 1854.
Abraham Meyers [signature]
J. S. Halsey [signature], Probate Judge

It is hereby Certified, That Abraham Myers and
Margaret Schuman were united together in marriage, on the
5th day of December 1854 by me,
M. Dahl

Clark county, Ohio
Filed and Recorded, Dec 13, 1854
J. S. Halsey [signature], Probate Judge
