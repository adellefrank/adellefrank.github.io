Education events at DrupalCon Portland 2013

 A list of education-related events happening at DrupalCon Portland. Let me know if I've missed any! Official hashtag for these sessions is: #drupaledu . New collaboration site for education users of drupal is: http://edudu.org (create an account and make comments).

Tuesday, May 21

  • 9:00-10:00am: How Oregon State University manages large scale Drupal (Room B 113)
  • 10:15-11:15am: Collaborative learning systems in Drupal: a case study (Room C 123)
  • 2:00-3:00pm: Unconsortium: the next phase of Drupal collaboration in higher education (Room/Hall A 1)
  • 3:15 BoF: Higher Education (Room A 107)
  • 8:00-11:00pm Social: Mediacurrent and ImageX Media After Party (206 SW Morrison St., Rock Bottom Brewery)

Wednesday, May 22

  • 10:45am BoF: Libraries (Room B 112)
  • 11:45am-1:00pm BoF: Higher Education Meetup (Room E 146)
  • 11:45am BoF: Content Strategy & Governance (Room B 112)
  • 11:45am BoF: Group Study Room Reservations for Colleges & Libraries (Room B 112)
  • 2:15pm BoF: Unlocking the power of Maestro for Business Workflow (Room A 108)
  • 3:45-4:45pm: Drupal based learning system for global health providers in developing countries (Room OR 203)
  • 3:45-4:45pm: User experience for online education (Room C 123)
  • 6:00-8:00pm Social: Drupal in Education Social (701 E. Burnside St., Burnside Brewing Company)
  • 8:00pm Social: Pantheon & New Relic Party (Jupiter Hotel, 800 East Burnside, Portland, OR 97214)

Thursday, May 23

  • 10:45am BoF: Teaching Drupal/CMS in universities (Room A 103)
  • 12:00pm BoF: Drupal as a Service (DaaS) in Higher Ed (Room A 108) 
  • 12:00pm BoF: Pantheon in Higher Education (Room B 111)
  • 1:00-2:00pm: Empowering Oregon K12 schools through Drupal & other FOSS tools (Room C 123)
  • 2:15pm BoF: The Debate: Subdomain vs. Domain (EDU sites & other) (Room A 108)
