Obituary of Reba Lazarus Bohrman from Florida Times-Union in 1973

Obituary for Reba (Lazarus) Bohrman in the Florida Times-Union newspaper of Jacksonville from 1973.

Citation

"Bohrman", funeral notice of Reba Lazarus, 29 November 1973 (Thursday), p. C13, col. 1; Florida Times-Union, newspaper; Jacksonville, Florida, United States.

Transcript

BOHRMAN - Funeral services for Mrs. Reba Lazarus Bohrman will be held at 3 p.m., Thursday [29 November] in the Chapel of Hardage-Krauss Northside Funeral Home, 34th and Main St., with Rabbi David Gaffney and the Rev. A. Marion officiating. Burial in Center Memorial Park.

Mrs. Bohrman, of 608 W. DeSoto St., Lake City, Fla., died Tuesday [27 November] after a brief illness, a native of Baltimore, sixty years. She was a member of the Jacksonville Jewish Center and a fifty year member of the Minnie Lee Chapter 113, Order of the Eastern Star in Lake City.

Survivors include two sons, Herman Bohrman, Lake City; and Fred Bohrman, of St. Louis, Mo.; a sister Mrs. Freda Rosenberg, of Jacksonville.

In lieu of flowers, memorials may be made to the donor's favorite charity.

Pallbearers will be active, Fred B. Bohrman, Irvin Bohrman, Leon Lazarus, Barry Kalin, Sam Rosenberg, and Louis Lazarus.
