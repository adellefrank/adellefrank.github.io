Delayed birth certificate of Carrie B. Long of Whitley, Indiana in 1942

Abstract of the delayed birth certificate of Carrie B. (Long) Pence, granted in Whitley, Indiana, United States in 1942 for her birth in 1888 in same county.

Citation

Delayed birth certificate of Carrie B. Pence, filed 2 October 1942, Whitley County Circuit Court (Indiana, United States); Delayed Birth Records, certificate #162-B; FamilySearch microfilm #1710716.

Abstract

  • Date granted: 2 October 1942
  • Full name: Carrie B. Pence
  • Birth date: 29 April 1888
  • Birth place: Whitley, Indiana
  • Color: white
  • Number of child to family: second
  • Midwife in attendance: Elsie Slessman
  • Father: A. M. Long (born Whitley, Indiana)
  • Mother: Sadie Slessman (born Richland, Ohio)
