Estate of James Hedges of Ohio, 1854-1858

An abstract of basic facts, as well as links to the estate records for General James Hedges of Mansfield, Richland, Ohio (United States), from 1854 through 1858. I have another blog post that gives an abstract and transcript of James' will.

  • Citations and Links
  • Administration (October 1854)
  • Docket (24 January 1855)
  • Inventory & Sale (13 January 1855)

Citations and Links

1. Will and administration record for James Hedges, filed 23 Oct 1854, Richland County Probate Court (Ohio, United States); Will Records, volume 1, citing original case #447 and will #146; images 307-310 of 677 (https://familysearch.org/pal:/MM9.3.1/TH-1961-31191-3974-2).

2. Court docket for estate #1366 of James Hedges, recorded 24 Jan 1855, Richland County Probate Court (Ohio, United States); Administration Docket, volume 1, page 430; image 231 of 615 (https://familysearch.org/pal:/MM9.3.1/TH-1942-31304-11866-96).

3. Estate inventory and sale record for James Hedges, filed 13 Jan 1855, Richland County Probate Court (Ohio, United States); Administration Record, volume 16, pages 332-341; images 191-197 of 684 (https://familysearch.org/pal:/MM9.3.1/TH-1951-31305-23652-79).

Administration record (October 1854)

The only information that seems distinct from the Will itself and other records, is:

  • Christian Hedges is absent in California (at this time).
  • Both Ellzey and Charles Hedges are Administrators.
  • Appraisers are: David Wise, William McIlvain, William Patterson.

Court Docket (24 January 1855)

1366 Estate of James Hedges, Deceased
BTW

  • Will admitted to probate vol 1/447 and letters testamentary issued Oct 23 A.D. 1854.
  • Inventory filed January 13th A.D. 1855.
  • Ellzey hedges resigned his trust of executor January 24th A.D. 1855, Jour vol. 1 page 5.

Bail:

  • Andrew S. Newman
  • John J. Todd
  • James Weldon
  • Solomon S. Lewis

Bond: $40.000.00

Inventory & Sale (13 January 1855)

[The date at the top of this page is 1858, similar to surrounding record dates, which seems late.]

Date property appraisal filed: 6 November 1854
Date of Sale: 17 November 1854 (after giving at least 15 days notice, held on premises of deceased's residence)
Bill of Sale filed: 15 December 1854
Date final Inventory filed: 13 January 1855 (Notary Public: Henry C. Hedges)
Total Appraised Value of Estate: $18,986.54
