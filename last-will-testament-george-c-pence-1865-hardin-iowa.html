Last Will and Testament of George C. Pence (d. 1865) of Hardin, Iowa

An abstract of basic facts, as well as downloadable PDF of the Will of George C. Pence (d. 1865) from Whitley county, Indiana and Hardin county, Iowa.

Citation

Will for George C. Pence, filed 4 September 1865, pp. 53-54, vol. B, Will Register, Hardin County, Iowa; FamilySearch MF #1547699 [Re-imaged copy of vol. B; downloadable PDF of Will].

Abstract of Will

An overview of the main facts - both dates and named individuals - found in this will.

Timeline

  • 26 June 1865: Will signed and witnessed
  • 4 September 1865: Date of Record (when Will was entered into probate by the court)
