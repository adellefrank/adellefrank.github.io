Estate of Peter Frank (Senior) of Adams, Indiana in 1875

An abstract of basic facts, as well as a downloadable PDF of three estate records for Peter Frank, Senior, of Adams county, Indiana in 1875 and 1878. I have another blog post that gives an abstract and transcript of Peter's will.

  • Citations and Files
  • Current Account (May 1875)
  • Final Account (October 1875)
  • Final Report (March 1878)

Citations and Files

1. Current Account of the Estate of Peter Frank Senior, filed 17 May 1875, May Term, Adams County Circuit Court; Probate Order Book 5, pages 191-192; microfilm #1-001-22, Indiana State Library (also FamilySearch MF #2322726). [Download 30MB PDF]

2. Final Account of the Estate of Peter Frank Senior, filed 12 October 1875, September Term, Adams County Circuit Court; Probate Order Book 5, pages 292-293; microfilm #1-001-22, Indiana State Library (also FamilySearch MF #2322726). [Download 28MB PDF]

3. Final Report of the Estate of Peter Frank Senior, filed 13 March 1878, February Term, Adams County Circuit Court; Probate Order Book 6, pages 239-240; microfilm #1-001-23, Indiana State Library (also FamilySearch MF #2322727). [Download 33MB PDF]
