Passport for Ernesto Luis Valera (y Rueda) of Cuba in 1961

Abstract of the passport for Ernesto Luis Valera y Rueda of Cuba, from 1961. Includes a summary of key information and a downloadable file of the scanned passport.

Citation and Download

Passport 44900 for Ernesto Luis Valera y Rueda, issued 22 June 1961, Department of Citizenship and Passports, Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Habana, Cuba. [Download the original document, scanned with permission from his daughter's private collection.]
