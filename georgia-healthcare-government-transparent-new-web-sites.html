Georgia healthcare & government go transparent with new web sites

Georgia's Government rolled out two new websites in January, aimed at making government and healthcare more transparent in Georgia:

  • Open.Georgia.gov: find out how the State is spending our tax dollars.
  • GeorgiaHealthInfo.gov: compare health insurance plans and research doctors and diseases.

  • State hosts sites to track finances, health (Brunswick News)
